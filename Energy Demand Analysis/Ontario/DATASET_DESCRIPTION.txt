ONTARIO HOURLY ELECTRICITY DEMAND 2002-2026

ABOUT IESO
The Independent Electricity System Operator (IESO) manages Ontario's electricity grid, balancing supply and demand in real-time. This dataset contains hourly electricity demand data from IESO spanning 2002-2026.

DATASET COLUMNS
Date: Day of measurement (YYYY-MM-DD)
Hour: Hour of day (1-24, where Hour 1 = 12am-1am). Eastern Standard Time year-round.
Ontario Demand: Average electricity consumption within Ontario during each hour, in MW (megawatts). Reflects actual local demand only.
Market Demand: Average electricity cleared through Ontario's wholesale market during each hour, in MW (megawatts). Includes exports to US and neighboring provinces. Typically 1-5% higher than Ontario Demand due to inter-provincial power flows.

DATA SOURCE
2002-2023: Kaggle dataset (jacobsharples/ontario-electricity-demand)
2024-2026: IESO direct reports consolidation
https://reports-public.ieso.ca/public/Demand/

USEFUL FOR
- Analyzing electricity consumption patterns and seasonal trends
- Grid capacity planning and forecasting
- Energy market analysis
- Time series analysis and machine learning
